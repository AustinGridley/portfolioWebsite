Rules of the game
You are trying to shoot your opponent. You can only shoot when you have bullets. When you reload, you gain one bullet. When you shield and your opponent shoots, you are safe and they lose a bullet. If you shoot without possessing any bullets, you automatically shield. If you both shoot, you both lose a bullet. Either one of you that is shot while reloading loses a life. First one with no lives loses. If you get five bullets you can super shot. If your opponent gets four bullets they can super shot. A super shot will automatically win the game. If both of you super shot, then you both will lose four and five bullets respectively.
